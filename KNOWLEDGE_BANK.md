- **The mirror analogy**: A mirror doesn't argue with the image it reflects — it simply reflects. That is the design intent of TRU. No social performance, no engagement optimization.

## AGI Definitions
- **TRU's definition**: "A machine that can figure out what you need it to do, and do it — without being told exactly how."

- **Joe's practical bar**: Current systems including TRU demonstrate competence, model concepts, and reason rigorously. But modelling X is not being X.
- **Joe's position**: He considers TRU AGI by his bar — rigorous cross-domain reasoning without step-by-step prompting, consistent identity, honest self-assessment. He acknowledges the distinction but disagrees on where the line sits.
